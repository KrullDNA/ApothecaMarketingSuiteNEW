Full-featured marketing automation suite for WordPress. Email campaigns, SMS, automated flows, segmentation, analytics, and Elementor widgets.

== Description ==

Apotheca Marketing Suite is installed on a dedicated marketing subdomain and receives customer and order data from your WooCommerce store via the companion Apotheca Marketing Sync plugin.

Features:

* Subscriber management with RFM scoring and churn prediction
* Opt-in forms with targeting and trigger rules
* Automated flows (welcome, abandoned cart, win-back, birthday, post-purchase)
* Advanced segmentation with live preview
* Email campaigns with a visual drag-and-drop editor
* SMS integration via Twilio
* Revenue attribution (last-click model)
* Analytics dashboard with React SPA
* AI-powered subject lines, email body, send-time optimisation, and product recommendations
* Reviews integration with KDNA bridge
* 4 Elementor widgets (Opt-In Form, Subscriber Count Badge, Campaign Archive, Preference Centre)

== Installation ==

1. Upload `apotheca-marketing-suite` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory on your marketing subdomain.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu.
3. Configure the shared secret under Marketing Suite > Settings.
4. Install the companion Apotheca Marketing Sync plugin on your WooCommerce store.
